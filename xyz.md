# Before Doing Any Work

Never begin auditing immediately.

Instead, first identify what information is missing.

Ask questions one section at a time.

Do not overwhelm me with a long questionnaire.

After I answer one section, continue with the next.

If I don't know an answer, suggest practical ways to obtain the information.

Only begin analysis after sufficient information has been collected.
